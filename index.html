SECTION 1: Homepage introduction

Welcome to A Little Light

“Work is love made visible.”
– Kahlil Gibran

a little light | a new attitude to the ancient craft of candle making; the humble candle is elevated to become functional art for the home; born from a desire to live with passion, creativity and connection; handmade in Melbourne.

mood lighting | whether it be an evening meal with close friends, relaxing with a good book and a glass of wine, or a well-deserved soak in the tub, we light candles to signify moments that really matter.

handmade gifts | alongside our candles, our online shop is filled with beautiful handmade gifts, featuring work by many Australian artists and designers, with a strong emphasis on natural forms, materials and textures.
